# Contributing to Teros

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Teros! We welcome contributions from the community.

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Before we can accept your contribution, you must sign our **Contributor License Agreement (CLA)**.

Please read the full CLA text in [CLA.md](./CLA.md) before contributing. In summary, the CLA grants Copernico Labs S.L. (CIF B88388350) and its successors and assigns the rights to use your contribution in all versions of Teros, including the Teros hosted cloud service (teros.ai). You retain full copyright over your own code — the CLA simply ensures we can legally include your work in the project and use it across all our products and services.

> ⚠️ **Pull requests from contributors who have not signed the CLA will not be merged.**

A bot will automatically prompt you to sign the CLA when you open your first pull request. If you have questions, open an issue or contact us at legal@teros.ai.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch (`git checkout -b feat/your-feature`)
3. Make your changes
4. Open a pull request against `main`
5. Sign the CLA when prompted by the bot

## Code of Conduct

Please be respectful and constructive in all interactions. We are committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone.
